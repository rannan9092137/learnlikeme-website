Privacy

This website is a static informational site. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and collects no personal data.

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log requests per Google's font privacy notes. If you email us, we use your message only to reply to you.

Photos of students on this site are published with the permission of our partner schools. If you have a concern about a photo, contact us and we will remove it promptly.
